Site Survey

Professional on-site evaluation. The foundation of every accurate solar proposal.

Pre-Survey Preparation

Complete these steps before arriving at the client's property

🛰️ Google Maps Satellite Review

  • Check roof orientation (ideal: south-facing or flat)
  • Estimate roof area from satellite view
  • Identify shading sources (trees, buildings, towers)
  • Note nearby structures and terrain
  • Verify address and access route
  • Pre-size system: estimate kWp potential

📝 Client Questionnaire (WhatsApp)

  • Request latest electricity bill photo
  • Confirm property ownership
  • Roof age and last maintenance
  • Planned renovations or expansions?
  • Confirm someone 18+ present
  • Preferred survey time slot

On-Site Survey Checklist

Complete every item — typically 45-60 minutes

📐 Roof Measurements

  • Total roof area (length × width)
  • Usable area (minus vents, edges)
  • Roof pitch angle (degrees)
  • Ridge and eave height
  • Number of roof planes

🧭 Tilt & Azimuth

  • Compass bearing of each face
  • Optimal: 0-15° tilt (8°N latitude)
  • Document deviations from optimal
  • East-west split for flat roofs

🌳 Shading Analysis

  • Morning shade: east (6-10 AM)
  • Midday: south (10 AM-2 PM)
  • Afternoon: west (2-6 PM)
  • Tree growth projection (5-10yr)
  • Shade % estimate per section

🏗️ Structural Condition

  • Roof material and condition
  • Leaks, rust, or damage signs
  • Rafter/purlin spacing
  • Load-bearing capacity
  • Remaining roof life estimate

⚡ Electrical Panel

  • Main breaker size (amps)
  • Available breaker slots
  • Panel brand and age
  • Wire gauge (copper/aluminum)
  • Grounding system type
  • Panel to inverter distance

📊 Meter & Service

  • ENSA meter number (NIS)
  • Single-phase or three-phase
  • Meter location accessibility
  • Transformer distance/capacity

Panama Roof Types

Pros and cons for each roof type common in the Azuero Peninsula

🏭

Metal (Zinc)

Most Common in Azuero
Pros
  • Fastest install (clamp mounting)
  • No roof penetrations
  • Lightweight mounting
  • Good heat dissipation
Cons
  • May need rust treatment
  • Shorter roof life (15-25yr)
  • Noise in heavy rain
🏢

Concrete (Losa)

Common in Urban Areas
Pros
  • Excellent structural capacity
  • Long roof life (40+ years)
  • Optimal tilt with ballast
  • Tilt frames for better angle
Cons
  • Waterproofing penetrations
  • Heavier mounting hardware
  • More labor-intensive
🏠

Clay/Concrete Tile

Premium Residences
Pros
  • Long-lasting (50+ years)
  • Aesthetically appealing
  • Good ventilation underneath
Cons
  • Most complex installation
  • Tiles may crack
  • Special tile hooks needed
  • Higher cost (+15-20%)
