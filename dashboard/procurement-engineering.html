String Sizing for Panama

Panama's tropical climate requires specific derating considerations for string sizing.

Ambient Temperature

Design for 35°C ambient. Cell temp: 55-65°C under full irradiance.

T_cell = T_amb + (NOCT - 20) × G/800

Voltage Derating

Voc drops at high temps. Jinko 580W: -0.29%/°C temp coefficient.

Voc_hot = Voc_STC × [1 + TC × (T_cell - 25)]

MPPT Voltage Range

Huawei 10KTL MPPT: 200-850V. Size for 25°C morning to 65°C afternoon.

Strings: 9-13 modules (Jinko 580W + Huawei 10KTL)

Derating Factors

Total system efficiency: ~83-85% after all derating in Panama.

η_system = η_temp × η_soil × η_mis × η_wire × η_inv
