Qualification Criteria

Quick-qualify leads within 5 minutes of first contact

Must Have (Qualify)

  • Monthly bill > $100/month
  • Owns property (or long-term lease)
  • No major shading (>80% sun exposure)
  • Roof in acceptable condition
  • ENSA service active

Nice to Have (Priority)

  • Bill > $200/month (higher ROI)
  • Metal roof (easiest install)
  • Decision-maker available
  • Timeline: within 3 months
  • Referral from existing customer

Disqualifiers

  • Bill < $80/month (ROI too low)
  • Renter without owner permission
  • Roof needs replacement
  • Heavy shading (trees/buildings)
  • No ENSA meter / illegal connection

Lead Scoring System

Categorize every lead for appropriate follow-up cadence

🔥 HOT
Score: 80-100 points

Ready to Buy

  • Monthly bill > $200
  • Owns property, ready now
  • Decision-maker engaged
  • Asked about pricing/timeline
  • Referral from existing client
Action: Call within 2 hours
🌤 WARM
Score: 40-79 points

Interested, Needs Nurturing

  • Monthly bill $100-200

  • Needs to discuss with spouse
  • Comparing options
  • Timeline: 3-6 months

Score: 0-39 points

Just Browsing

  • Monthly bill < $100
  • Renter or uncertain ownership
  • No clear timeline
  • Just gathering information
  • Price-shopping only
