Section 03

Ley 417 — Tax Incentives

Panama's Ley 417 (December 2023) is a game-changer for solar adoption, reducing effective equipment costs by 12-15% and making the full investment deductible from income tax.

100% Import Duty Exemption

Panels, inverters, batteries, mounting systems, and cables — all duty-free.

100% ITBMS Exemption

7% VAT equivalent eliminated on all solar equipment purchases.

Income Tax Deduction

Full investment is deductible — effectively 15-25% cost reduction for businesses.
