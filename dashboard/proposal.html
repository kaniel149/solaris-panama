TAX BENEFITS

Ley 417 Breakdown

Panama's Law 417 (December 2023) provides powerful incentives that make solar the smartest investment in Central America.

Ley 417 — Your Benefits

🏷️

100% Tax Deduction

Deduct the entire cost of your solar system from taxable income in the year of installation.

🚢

Import Duty Exemption

Zero import taxes on solar panels, inverters, and related equipment. Savings of 7-15% on equipment cost.

⚡

Net Metering

Excess energy sent to the grid earns credits on your electricity bill. Your meter literally runs backwards.

📋

ASEP Fast-Track

Simplified permitting process through ASEP for systems under 500kWp. We handle all paperwork.
