Maintenance: The Compounding Engine

While installation margins are solid at 20%, maintenance contracts at ~90% margin create predictable recurring revenue. By Month 12, maintenance alone generates $2,900/month — growing every month as new installations come online. This is the long-term value builder.

Cost Structure

Where the Money Goes

Equipment — 60%

Panels (LONGi, Jinko, Trina), inverters (Huawei SUN2000), mounting systems, cables. Imported via Colón Free Zone. Ley 417 eliminates import duties and VAT.

Labor — 20%

Installation crew, electrical work, structural mounting. Initially subcontracted, transitioning to in-house team by Month 6.

Marketing — 10%

Facebook/Instagram ads (Azuero targeting), Google Business Profile, content creation, referral bonuses. Zero digital competition = low CPA.

Overhead — 10%

Vehicle, insurance, tools, office costs, ASEP licensing, professional certifications, accounting.
