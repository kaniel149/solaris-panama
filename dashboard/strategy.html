LAW 417

Incentivos para Energía Solar

Panama's Law 417 is the most aggressive solar incentive in Central America. It fundamentally changes the economics of every sale.

100% Tax Deduction

The full cost of solar equipment and installation can be deducted from taxable income. For a commercial client paying $50,000 for solar, that's a $50,000 tax write-off. At Panama's 25% corporate tax rate, this means the government effectively pays 25% of the system.

Import Duty Exemption

All solar equipment — panels, inverters, mounting systems, cables — enters Panama duty-free under Law 417. This saves 7-15% on equipment costs compared to importing without the exemption, making our pricing even more competitive.

TARGET SEGMENTS

Who We Serve

🏠 Expat Homes

American & European homeowners in Pedasí, Venao, Cañas. 5-15kW systems. $6K-$20K. Environmentally conscious, high willingness to pay.

🏨 Hotels

Boutique eco-hotels and surf resorts. 20-100kW systems. $20K-$120K. Marketing angle: "powered by solar" attracts eco-tourists.

🍽️ Restaurants

Bars, restaurants, beach clubs. 10-30kW. $10K-$40K. High electricity use (refrigeration, AC). Quick payback.

🌾 Farms

Cattle ranches (ganadería), agricultural operations. 15-50kW. $15K-$60K. Water pumping and cold storage loads.

🏢 Commercial

Warehouses, offices, retail. 50-300kW. $50K-$360K. Law 417 tax benefits most impactful here. Longest contracts.

PRICING

Pricing Strategy

Competitive pricing with healthy margins. Position as premium but fair.

Residential

$1.10 – $1.30/Wp

Typical system: 8kW = $8,800 – $10,400. Includes panels, inverter, mounting, installation, ASEP permitting. Payback: 3–3.5 years at current rates.

Commercial

$1.00 – $1.20/Wp

Typical system: 50kW = $50,000 – $60,000. Volume pricing. Law 417 tax deduction saves 25% (at corporate rate). Effective cost after tax benefit: $37K-$45K. Payback: 2.5–3 years.
