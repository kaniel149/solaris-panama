REVENUE MODEL

Three Revenue Streams

Installation revenue drives growth. Maintenance contracts build recurring income. Commercial projects deliver scale.

🏠

Residential

$8,000–$15,000

Per project. Typical 5-15kW systems for expat homes and local residences. Average deal size: $12,000. Fastest to close, highest volume.

🏨

Commercial

$20,000–$80,000

Per project. Hotels, restaurants, farms, and large properties. Average deal: $45,000. Longer sales cycle but higher margins and stronger social proof.

🔧

Maintenance

$50–$200/mo

Per system. Annual cleaning, monitoring, inverter checks, and performance reports. Builds to $3K+/month recurring by year-end with 20+ active systems.
