PERSONA 1
🏡

The Expat Homeowner

American/European retiree in Pedasí or Playa Venao
DEMOGRAPHICS

Age 55–75. Retired or semi-retired. Relocated from US/Canada/Europe. Owns a $200K–$600K home. Eco-conscious, values independence and quality of life.

MONTHLY ELECTRICITY
$800 – $2,000

😩 Pain Points

  • Electricity bills are 3–5x what they paid back home
  • Frequent power outages disrupt daily life
  • Running AC nonstop in tropical heat
  • Unfamiliar with local contractors, worried about scams
TOP OBJECTION
"Will solar actually work here? I've never seen panels in Azuero."
WINNING MESSAGE
Azuero has 5.3 peak sun hours — more than most of Florida. Your system pays for itself in 2.5 years, then delivers free electricity for 25+. We handle everything: permits, installation, net metering setup. Professional team with Israeli solar industry experience.

🏨

The Boutique Hotel Owner

Eco-tourism resort, 10–50 rooms in the Azuero coast
DEMOGRAPHICS

Age 35–55. Entrepreneur or investor. Often international (European, Israeli, American). Sustainability is a brand value, not just a buzzword. Competes on experience and eco-credentials.

MONTHLY ELECTRICITY
$2,000 – $8,000

😩 Pain Points

  • Electricity is the #1 operating expense
  • Guests expect sustainability, competitors are marketing it
  • Power outages = bad reviews on TripAdvisor/Booking
  • Generators are noisy, expensive, and ruin the vibe
TOP OBJECTION
"Will installation disrupt our guests? We can't shut down."
WINNING MESSAGE
We install in 3–5 days with zero downtime for your guests. Roof work happens during checkout hours. Plus — 'Powered by Solar' becomes your marketing advantage on Booking.com. Eco-conscious travelers pay 15–20% more for sustainable stays. Your panels pay for themselves AND attract premium guests.

PERSONA 3
🍽️

The Restaurant & Bar Owner

Beach bar, restaurant, or café with heavy AC and refrigeration
DEMOGRAPHICS

Age 30–55. Local Panamanian or expat entrepreneur. Hands-on operator, works 12+ hour days. Tight margins, every dollar counts. Pragmatic — needs proof, not promises.

MONTHLY ELECTRICITY
$500 – $3,000

😩 Pain Points

  • Walk-in coolers and AC run 24/7, bills are brutal
  • Power outages spoil inventory ($500+ in food loss)
  • Seasonal revenue swings make fixed costs painful
  • Cash flow is king — upfront costs feel risky
TOP OBJECTION
"I can't afford $10K+ upfront right now. Business is seasonal."
WINNING MESSAGE
At $2,000/month in electricity, a $12K system pays for itself in 2.5 years. That's $18,000 in savings over just 5 years. Under Ley 417, you deduct the full cost from your business taxes — so you save twice. Plus: no more spoiled inventory during outages. Your coolers stay cold, your margins stay healthy.

PERSONA 4
🐄

The Farm & Ranch Owner

Ganadería — large property with irrigation, pumps, and cold storage
DEMOGRAPHICS

Age 40–70. Multi-generational Panamanian landowner or expat farmer. Practical, traditional values. Respects results over marketing. Often has 10+ hectares with barns, equipment sheds, and processing facilities.

MONTHLY ELECTRICITY
$300 – $1,500

😩 Pain Points

  • Irrigation pumps cost a fortune to run on grid power
  • Remote location = poor grid connection and frequent outages
  • Cold storage for milk/meat/produce is essential but expensive
  • Diesel generators are the backup — loud, dirty, costly
TOP OBJECTION
"Solar doesn't work for farms. My equipment needs too much power."
WINNING MESSAGE
You have the biggest advantage of all: roof space. Your barns, sheds, and equipment buildings are perfect for panels. Solar pumps your water during the sunniest hours — exactly when your crops need it most. We've designed systems for Israeli farms with the same needs. Reliable, simple, and it pays for itself while your diesel generator sits unused.

🏢

The Commercial Property

Warehouse, office building, or shopping plaza — ROI-driven decision maker
DEMOGRAPHICS

Age 40–65. Business owner, property manager, or investment group. Data-driven, thinks in spreadsheets and ROI. May require board/partner approval. Values professional documentation and financial projections.

MONTHLY ELECTRICITY
$3,000 – $15,000

😩 Pain Points

  • Electricity is eating into property margins and tenant satisfaction
  • Large flat roofs sitting unused = wasted asset
  • Rising energy costs with no hedge strategy
  • Competing properties are going green to attract tenants
TOP OBJECTION
"I need board approval. Show me the numbers that will convince my partners."
WINNING MESSAGE
Here's what your board needs to see: a 300kW system at $250K generates $1.73M in savings over 25 years — that's a 690% ROI. Payback in 3.5 years. Full Ley 417 tax deduction in year one. We provide a complete financial case study with your specific numbers, ready for your next board meeting. Your unused roof becomes your highest-performing asset.
