PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography Style

Tropical but not touristy. Natural light, golden hour preferred. Architecture matters.

✓ Ideal Subjects

Solar panels on beautiful buildings — beach houses, boutique hotels, modern homes. The Azuero coastline. Installation crews at work (professional, safety-focused). Happy clients.

✓ Color Treatment

Warm color temperature with a slight golden cast. High contrast, not flat. Shadows should be rich, not crushed. Never over-saturated — keep it natural.

✗ Never Use

No stock photos of generic solar farms in deserts. No cheesy thumbs-up or handshake photos. No cold/blue color grading. No drone shots that make panels look like an afterthought.

Bilingual Notes

All client-facing materials in English and Spanish. Spanish should feel native, not translated. Use "usted" form (professional, respectful). Key terms: energía solar, paneles solares, independencia energética.

GUIDELINES

Do's and Don'ts

✓ Do

Use generous whitespace — let the design breathe
Pair Solaris Green with Solar Gold sparingly
Use warm, golden-toned photography
Maintain consistent 8px spacing grid
Cards: 12px radius · Buttons: 6px · Badges: pill
Reference Law 417 in commercial materials

✗ Don't

Use more than 3 colors in a single layout
Center-align body text
Use gradients (subtle hero backgrounds excepted)
Mix brand fonts with system fonts
Use pure black (#000) — use Charcoal (#1A1A1A)
Overuse gold — it's an accent, not a primary
