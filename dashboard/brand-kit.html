PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography Style Guide

Warm golden hour aesthetic. Tropical but not touristy. The real Panama — lush, warm, authentic.

✓ Do

  • Natural light, golden hour preferred
  • Solar panels on beautiful buildings (beach houses, hotels, modern homes)
  • People in context: homeowners, installers at work
  • Frame panels as a design element, not an eyesore
  • Warm color temperature, slight golden cast
  • High contrast, rich shadows (not crushed)

✕ Don't

  • No stock photos of generic solar farms in deserts
  • No cheesy thumbs-up or handshake photos
  • No cold/blue color grading
  • No drone shots that make panels look like an afterthought
  • Never over-saturated — natural palette only
