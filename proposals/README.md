# Solaris Energy S.A. — Proposal Templates

Professional solar energy proposal templates for the Panamanian market.  
Adapted from Copenhagen Solar (TM Energy) templates to Panama's legal and regulatory framework.

## Templates

| File | Type | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| `commercial-proposal-template.html` | Commercial | Full proposal for hotels, restaurants, and commercial properties. 3 options: EPC, PPA, EPC+Battery. Includes comparison table, agreement section with signatures. |
| `residential-proposal-template.html` | Residential | Streamlined proposal for homes, villas, and PH apartments. 2 options: Direct Purchase (EPC) and EPC+Battery. Simpler layout, family-focused messaging. |
| `financing-options.html` | Financing | Slide-deck style presentation covering financing models, bank ranking, Ley 417 benefits, ROI calculations, and ASEP regulatory framework. Use as a sales tool or client education resource. |

## How to Use

1. **Copy the template** for each new proposal
2. **Replace all `[bracketed placeholders]`** with actual project data
3. **Update the reference number** (e.g., `SE-2026-002`)
4. **Add project images** to the proposal directory
5. **Print to PDF** or share as HTML link

## Panama Regulatory Notes

- **ASEP** — Autoridad Nacional de los Servicios Públicos (utility regulator)
- **Ley 417 (2019)** — Renewable energy incentives law
  - 100% income tax exemption on solar generation
  - 100% customs/import duty exemption on solar equipment
  - Net Metering available (up to 500 kW)
  - ITBMS (VAT) exemption on solar equipment
- **Typical ASEP approval timeline:** 2-4 weeks
- **Grid connection:** Bidirectional meter required for Net Metering
