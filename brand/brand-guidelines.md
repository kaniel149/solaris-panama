## 1. Brand Overview

**Solaris Energy** is a premium solar rooftop installation company based in the Azuero Peninsula, Panama. We serve commercial and residential clients — from expat homeowners to hotel operators — with clean energy solutions in one of the most sun-rich regions on earth.

**Tagline:** OWN YOUR ENERGY

**Brand Promise:** Professional solar expertise delivered with tropical warmth. We make going solar feel premium, simple, and inevitable.

**Tone Keywords:** Confident · Clean · Warm · Expert · Approachable

## 4. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
Sun-over-mountain silhouette in circular badge format. "SOLARIS" text below icon, "OWN YOUR ENERGY" tagline beneath.

### Variations
1. **Full badge** — Circle with icon + text + tagline (primary use)
2. **Wordmark only** — "SOLARIS" in Playfair Display 700, tracked out
3. **Icon only** — Sun-mountain silhouette (for favicons, app icons, small formats)

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space = height of the "S" in SOLARIS on all sides.

### Minimum Sizes
- Full badge: 48px height minimum (digital), 15mm (print)
- Icon only: 24px height minimum (digital), 8mm (print)

### Approved Color Combinations
| Logo | Background |
|------|-----------|
| Pearl White | Solaris Green |
| Solaris Green | Pearl White / Warm Sand |
| Solar Gold | Solaris Green |

### Logo Don'ts
- ✗ Do not stretch or distort
- ✗ Do not rotate
- ✗ Do not add drop shadows or effects
- ✗ Do not place on busy photographic backgrounds without overlay
- ✗ Do not change the typeface
- ✗ Do not rearrange elements
- ✗ Do not use at less than minimum size

## 5. Photography Style

### Aesthetic
- **Natural light preferred.** Golden hour is our sweet spot.
- **Tropical but not touristy.** Show the real Panama — lush, warm, authentic.
- **People in context.** Show homeowners on their roofs, business owners in their properties, installers at work.
- **Architecture matters.** Frame solar panels as a design element, not an eyesore.

### Subject Guidelines
- Solar panels on beautiful buildings (beach houses, boutique hotels, modern homes)
- The Azuero coastline and landscape
- Installation crews at work (professional, safety-focused)
- Happy clients in their properties
- Close-up details: panels, inverters, clean wiring

### Color Treatment
- Warm color temperature. Slight golden cast.
- High contrast, not flat. Shadows should be rich, not crushed.
- Never over-saturated. Natural palette.

### Don'ts
- ✗ No stock photos of generic solar farms in deserts
- ✗ No cheesy thumbs-up or handshake photos
- ✗ No cold/blue color grading
- ✗ No drone shots that make panels look like an afterthought

## 6. Tone of Voice

### Personality
We speak like a **knowledgeable friend who happens to be a solar expert**. Not a salesman. Not a textbook. A trusted advisor.

### Principles

**1. Confident, not arrogant**
- ✓ "We've designed the optimal system for your property."
- ✗ "We're the best solar company in Panama."

**2. Clear, not dumbed down**
- ✓ "Your system will produce 1,200 kWh per month — enough to cover your entire electricity bill."
- ✗ "Solar panels make electricity from the sun!"

**3. Warm, not casual**
- ✓ "Let's find the right solution for your home."
- ✗ "Yo, ready to go solar??"

**4. Premium, not exclusive**
- ✓ "Invest in your property's energy independence."
- ✗ "For discerning clients only."

### Bilingual Considerations
- All client-facing materials should work in English and Spanish
- Spanish copy should feel native, not translated
- Use "usted" form in Spanish (professional, respectful)
- Key terms: energía solar, paneles solares, independencia energética

## 7. Brand Applications — Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- ✓ Use generous whitespace — let the design breathe
- ✓ Pair Solaris Green with Solar Gold sparingly (gold is the spice, not the dish)
- ✓ Use photography with warm, golden tones
- ✓ Maintain consistent 8px spacing grid
- ✓ Round corners at 8px for cards, 4px for buttons, full-round for badges
- ✓ Use subtle shadows (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06))
- ✓ Reference Law 417 (Incentivos para Energía Solar) in commercial materials

### Don't
- ✗ Use more than 3 colors in a single layout
- ✗ Center-align body text
- ✗ Use gradients (exception: subtle background transitions on hero sections)
- ✗ Use clip art or emoji as design elements
- ✗ Mix brand fonts with system fonts
- ✗ Use pure black (#000000) — always use Charcoal (#1A1A1A)
- ✗ Use pure white (#FFFFFF) — use Pearl White (#FEFDFB)
- ✗ Overuse gold — it's an accent, not a primary

## 8. Spacing & Layout

### Grid
- 8px base unit
- Common spacing: 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128
- Max content width: 1200px
- Section padding: 96px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)

### Border Radius
- Cards: 12px
- Buttons: 6px
- Input fields: 6px
- Badges/tags: 100px (pill)
- Images: 8px

### Shadows
- **Subtle:** `0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.04)`
- **Card:** `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- **Elevated:** `0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- **Modal:** `0 16px 48px rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`
