# Channel Strategy — Solaris Panama

## Reality Check: Rural Panama B2B

Azuero Peninsula is relationship-driven. Cold digital ads alone won't work. Trust is built through personal connections, local institutions, and word-of-mouth. Strategy must be **hybrid: digital + on-the-ground.**

---

## Tier 1 — High Priority (Start Week 1)

### 1. WhatsApp Business + Groups
- **Why:** Everyone in Panama uses WhatsApp for business. It IS the CRM.
- **How:**
  - WhatsApp Business profile with catalog (services, photos, pricing ranges)
  - Join local business WhatsApp groups (Chitré, Las Tablas, Pedasí business communities)
  - Create a broadcast list for leads
  - Share bite-sized content: Law 417 savings tips, electricity cost comparisons
- **Cost:** Free

### 2. Personal Network & Referrals
- **Why:** In Azuero, "who sent you" matters more than any ad
- **How:**
  - Identify 10 connected locals (real estate agents, architects, accountants)
  - Offer referral fee: $500 per closed commercial deal
  - Ask every client for 3 introductions
- **Cost:** Performance-based only

### 3. Facebook (Organic + Paid)
- **Why:** Panama's #1 social platform. Business owners 35-60 are active daily.
- **How:**
  - Facebook Business Page: Solaris Panamá
  - Join and post in: "Negocios en Azuero", "Empresarios de Chitré", "Pedasí Expats" (for resort owners)
  - Paid: $10-20/day targeted ads — Azuero, business owners, 30-65, interests: business/agriculture
  - Content: Before/after, ROI calculators, Law 417 explainers
- **Cost:** $300-600/month paid + organic

### 4. Cámara de Comercio (Chamber of Commerce)
- **Why:** Direct access to every serious business owner in the region
- **How:**
  - Join Cámara de Comercio de Herrera and Los Santos
  - Attend monthly meetings, offer to present on Law 417
  - Sponsor a breakfast event: "Cómo Reducir su Factura Eléctrica un 70%"
- **Cost:** Membership ~$100-300/year + event ~$200-500

---

## Tier 2 — Medium Priority (Start Week 3-4)

### 5. Radio (Local Stations)
- **Why:** Farmers and rural business owners listen to radio daily (driving, working)
- **Stations:** Radio Azuero, Radio Herrera, local AM stations
- **How:**
  - 30-second spots during morning business hours (6-9 AM)
  - Sponsorship of local news segment
  - Script: "¿Sabía que la Ley 417 le permite instalar paneles solares sin pagar impuestos?"
- **Cost:** $200-500/month for regular spots

### 6. Rotary / Lions Club
- **Why:** Business elite. Decision-makers. Trust circles.
- **How:**
  - Join Rotary Club of Chitré
  - Give a presentation on solar + sustainability
  - Build relationships over months, not weeks
- **Cost:** Membership ~$200-400/year

### 7. Local Newspapers
- **Papers:** La Prensa (national, Azuero section), El Siglo, local newsletters
- **How:**
  - Advertorial: "Cómo Empresas en Azuero Ahorran con Energía Solar"
  - Press release when first installation completes
- **Cost:** $200-800 per placement

---

## Tier 3 — Build Over Time

### 8. Google Search (SEO + Ads)
- **Why:** Low search volume now, but capture intent when it exists
- **Keywords:** "paneles solares Azuero", "energía solar comercial Panamá", "Ley 417 paneles solares"
- **How:** Landing page optimized for Spanish keywords, Google My Business listing
- **Cost:** SEO = time; Ads = $200-400/month when ready

### 9. LinkedIn
- **Why:** Hotel chain owners, supermarket executives may be on LinkedIn
- **How:** Kaniel's personal profile → thought leadership on commercial solar in LatAm
- **Cost:** Free (time only)

### 10. Events & Ferias
- **Feria de Azuero** (late April) — huge regional event, booth opportunity
- **Agricultural expos** — target large farm owners
- **Cost:** Booth $500-2,000 depending on event

## Key Principle

**In Azuero: Relationships > Reach.** One lunch with the president of Cámara de Comercio de Herrera is worth more than 10,000 Facebook impressions. Digital supports; personal sells.
