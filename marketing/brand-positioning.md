# Brand Positioning — Solaris Panama

## Name Options

| Name | Rationale | Domain Check Needed |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Solaris Panamá** | Solar + prestige, easy to remember in Spanish | solarispanama.com |
| **Solaris Azuero** | Hyper-local positioning, owns the region | solarisazuero.com |
| **Sol del Sur** | "Sun of the South" — poetic, Panamanian feel | soldelsur.com |
| **AzueroSol** | Fuses region + sun, unique | azuerosol.com |

**Recommendation:** **Solaris Panamá** — scalable beyond Azuero, professional, memorable. Use "Energía Solar para Azuero" as a descriptor line.

## Taglines (Spanish-first)

1. **"Su techo ya genera dinero. Solo falta activarlo."** (Your roof already makes money. Just activate it.)
2. **"Energía solar comercial. Sin competencia. Sin complicaciones."** (Commercial solar. No competition. No hassle.)
3. **"El sol de Azuero trabaja para usted."** (Azuero's sun works for you.)
4. **"Ley 417 + su techo = ahorro garantizado."** (Law 417 + your roof = guaranteed savings.)

**Recommendation:** Tagline #1 for brand. #4 for performance ads.

## Positioning Statement

> For hotel owners, supermarket operators, and large agricultural businesses in Azuero Peninsula who pay $2,000+/month in electricity, Solaris Panamá is the only local solar installer that delivers turnkey commercial rooftop systems with guaranteed ROI — backed by Law 417 tax exemptions and international engineering expertise from Israel's leading solar market.

## Key Differentiators

1. **First mover in Azuero** — zero rooftop solar competition in the region
2. **Law 417 specialist** — we handle the paperwork for tax + import duty exemptions
3. **International expertise** — Kaniel's Navitas/Israel background (Israel = #1 per-capita solar)
4. **Commercial-only focus** — not residential hobbyists; we do 400m²+ roofs
5. **Local presence** — we're in Azuero, not flying in from Panama City

## Brand Personality

- **Professional but approachable** — business owners, not engineers
- **Confident** — first mover advantage, state it
- **Educational** — most prospects don't know Law 417 benefits
- **Spanish-first** — all materials in Spanish, English as secondary

## Visual Direction

- Colors: Deep blue + solar gold/amber + white
- Style: Clean, professional, photos of Azuero landscapes + commercial roofs
- Logo: Sun motif integrated with roof silhouette
- Avoid: Overly techy, green-washing clichés, stock photos of foreign buildings
