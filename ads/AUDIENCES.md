## Audience 4: Lookalike from Website Visitors

**Source:** Meta Pixel on kaniel149.github.io/solaris-panama/
**Seed:** All website visitors, last 180 days
**Lookalike %:** 1% (Panama)
**Country:** Panama

**Note:** This audience will become available once the pixel has collected at least 100 visitors. Start with 1% lookalike, expand to 2-3% in Month 2 if needed.

**Additional Retargeting Audiences (Custom):**
- Website visitors (last 30 days) — exclude converters
- Facebook/Instagram page engagers (last 30 days)
- Ad video viewers (50%+ watched, last 14 days)
- WhatsApp conversation starters who didn't convert

## Audience Layering Strategy

**Month 1 (Testing):**
- Run Audiences 1, 2, 3 separately in ad sets
- $5-10/day per ad set
- Let Meta optimize delivery
- Identify which audience has lowest cost per WhatsApp conversation

**Month 2 (Optimization):**
- Kill bottom-performing audience
- Scale top 2 audiences
- Launch Audience 4 (Lookalike) once pixel has data
- Begin retargeting campaigns

**Month 3 (Scale):**
- Consolidate into 2-3 winning audiences
- Expand location radius for winners
- Create 2% and 3% lookalikes
- Test broad targeting with creative as the filter
