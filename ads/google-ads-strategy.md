## Display Campaign

### Targeting
**Audience Segments:**
- In-market: Solar energy products, Home improvement, Commercial property
- Affinity: Green living enthusiasts, Property investors, Business owners
- Custom intent: People searching for "panama real estate", "pedasi property", "electricity panama"

**Placement Targeting:**
- International Living website
- Panama expat forums
- Real estate portals (Encuentra24, Compreoalquile)
- Travel/lifestyle blogs mentioning Pedasi/Venao

**Geo:** Panama (primary), US/Canada/UK (secondary — targeting people researching Panama)

### Display Ad Sizes
- 300x250 (medium rectangle) — primary
- 728x90 (leaderboard)
- 160x600 (wide skyscraper)
- 320x50 (mobile banner)
- Responsive display ads (primary — let Google optimize)

### Display Creative
- Hero: Aerial solar roof shot with Pedasi ocean backdrop
- Text: "Your Roof Could Save You 50-70% | Tax-Free Solar (Law 417)"
- CTA: "Free Assessment"

## Remarketing Setup

### Audience Lists
1. **All website visitors** (540 days) — broad retargeting
2. **Landing page visitors** (90 days) — didn't convert
3. **Assessment form starters** (30 days) — abandoned form
4. **Calculator users** (90 days) — used savings calculator but didn't submit
5. **Blog readers** (180 days) — read Law 417 or solar content

### Remarketing Ad Strategy
| Audience | Message | CTA |
|----------|---------|-----|
| Page visitors (no conversion) | "Still thinking about solar? Your free assessment is waiting." | Get Assessment |
| Form abandoners | "You're 1 step away from seeing your savings." | Complete Assessment |
| Calculator users | "You saw the numbers. Ready to make it real?" | Schedule Consultation |
| Blog readers | "50-70% savings isn't a dream — it's Law 417." | Learn More |

### RLSA (Remarketing Lists for Search Ads)
- Bid +50% on search ads for users who already visited the website
- Show different ad copy: "Welcome back — your free assessment is ready"
