## Recommended Procurement Strategy

### For 70 kWp Commercial Systems
| Component | Recommended Source | Est. Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Panels (Tier 1: JA Solar, Canadian Solar, Jinko) | Meico Solar or Solarcellpa | $0.25-0.35/W |
| Inverters (Fronius, SMA, Huawei) | SiempreSol or Solarcellpa | $0.08-0.12/W |
| Mounting (aluminum, galvanized steel) | The Sol Patch or direct import | $0.05-0.08/W |
| BOS (cables, combiner boxes, disconnect) | The Sol Patch or Ab Corp | $0.03-0.05/W |
| **Total equipment** | | **$0.41-0.60/W** |

### Import Considerations
- **Law 417:** Zero import duty + zero 7% ITBMS = ~15-20% savings vs. pre-2024
- **Shipping:** Miami → Panama City by sea = 3-5 days
- **Customs:** Simplified with Law 417 exemption documentation
- **Minimum orders:** Check with Meico Solar for container pricing (usually 1x40ft = ~100-150 kWp of panels)
