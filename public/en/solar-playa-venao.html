Why Solar Here

Why solar makes sense in Playa Venao

The same sun that powers the surf economy can power your property — and here it shows up more than five hours a day at full strength.

Sun That Works Year-Round

Playa Venao gets 5+ peak sun-hours daily on Panama's dry Azuero coast. Production stays strong through the green season too — most rain falls in the afternoon, after your panels have already done a full morning's work.

Big Daytime Loads = Big Savings

Tax Incentives

Law 417: solar incentives that work for foreign owners

Panama's clean-energy law rewards whoever owns the property or business — your nationality doesn't matter.

What Law 417 means for a Venao property owner

  • Solar equipment enters Panama exempt from import duties and the 7% ITBMS sales tax — that discount is already in your quote
  • If your lodge, hostel, restaurant or rental produces Panama-source income, the solar investment can be deducted from Panamanian income tax (ISR)
  • Works for residents and non-resident owners alike — the benefit follows the property or business, not your visa
  • Surplus energy exported to the grid earns credits on your utility bill under net metering
  • Solaris prepares and files all Law 417 paperwork at no extra cost

Straight talk: the equipment exemptions lower the price for every buyer. The income-tax deduction only matters if you (or your Panamanian corporation) pay income tax here — which most Venao business owners do. If you're a non-resident with no Panama income, you still win on system cost, net metering and property value. We'll map your exact benefits before you commit.

Net Metering

Net metering, without the jargon

Panama's net metering program (medición neta) connects your solar system to the grid through a bidirectional meter. During the day your panels power the property and export whatever's left over; at night you draw from the grid as usual. At the end of the month you're billed on the difference — and if you exported more than you imported, the surplus rolls forward as a credit.

We size the system to your real consumption, and we handle the interconnection application with the utility from start to finish.
